About Ballooning

Hot Air Ballooning is a gentle experience that is suitable for all. From age 5 to our eldest passenger of 97 years this is an experience for everyone.

It is an outdoor activity that is weather dependent. South Africa's calm early mornings have previously allowed us to fly with a high success rate.

We fly 7 days a week and all year round.
